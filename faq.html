Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about bookings, tickets, payments, and account support.

Book It is a mobile app that lets you discover events, purchase tickets, and store them securely on your device. It’s designed to make attending concerts, festivals, sports, and local events simple and stress‑free.

You can sign up using your email address, phone number, or a supported social login. Once registered, you’ll be able to browse events, save favourites, and manage your tickets.

Browse events, select the one you want, choose your ticket type and quantity, then complete checkout using your preferred payment method. Your tickets will appear instantly in your “My Tickets” section.

All purchased tickets are stored in the My Tickets tab. You can view them anytime, even offline, and present them at the event for entry.

Book It supports major debit/credit cards, digital wallets, and other region‑specific payment options. Availability may vary depending on your location.
